About MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of plant life, art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating living collections, we invite visitors to discover how the botanical world shapes culture, science, and everyday life.

Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our galleries offer new perspectives on ecology, biodiversity, and digital innovation. Our botanical spaces span over 12,345 sq ft of living art, housing more than 1,234 plant species from across the globe. Every visit is unique — the living collections grow and change with the seasons.
