Exhibition Details

The Living Canopy spans 8,000 sq ft across two gallery floors. Using over 400 living plant species, spatial audio recorded in the Amazon basin, and programmable LED canopy lighting, the installation responds dynamically to visitor movement.

"I wanted visitors to feel genuinely small — in the best possible way. The canopy reminds us that human experience is one thin layer in a much older, more complex system." — Jayden Lee, Lead Curator.

Allow at least 90 minutes for The Living Canopy. Wear comfortable shoes — some sections have uneven terrain underfoot. Photography is welcome without flash. Guided tours depart at 11 AM and 2 PM daily.

All galleries are wheelchair accessible. Audio descriptions are available via the MonoMuse mobile app. Large-print exhibition guides are available at the front desk. Tactile plant specimens are in Gallery 2.
