Getting Here

🅿️

Parking

The Forbes Ave Garage (5-min walk) charges $8/day on weekends. Metered street parking is available on Botanical Way.

🚌

Public Transit

Port Authority buses 61A, 61B, and 61C stop one block away on Forbes Ave. Steel Plaza T Station is a 12-min walk.

♿

Accessibility

Accessible entrances on main and side doors. Accessible parking reserved in Forbes Ave Garage, Level 1.

Plan Your Visit

The Forbes Ave Garage is closest - a 5-minute walk. Street parking is metered on weekdays and free on Sundays after 6 PM.

Yes. All galleries are wheelchair accessible via wide doorways and lifts. Accessible restrooms are on every floor. Contact us in advance for additional arrangements.

Photography for personal use is welcome throughout the museum without flash. Tripods and commercial photography require prior written permission.

Sealed water bottles are permitted in all galleries. Food is restricted to the museum café and outdoor terrace. No hot beverages in the living plant galleries.
